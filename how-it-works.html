How It Works

Order online and receive a certified date-of-death appraisal in three to five business days.

Home / How It Works

From Order to Delivery

Simple, fast, and entirely online.

1

Submit Your Request

Provide the property address, the date of death, and your contact information through the online order form. Tell us the purpose — step-up basis, Form 706, trust distribution, or probate.

2

Review & Invoice

We review the property and the assignment and email a confirmation with a secure invoice, typically within one business day. Your exact fee is confirmed before you commit.

3

Pay & Share Any Photos

Pay the invoice online. If the property’s condition as of the date of death matters to the value, you can upload photographs or documentation for us to consider.

4

Analysis

We research the MLS, public records, and market trends specific to the property’s Orange County submarket to identify the best comparable sales as of the date of death, and reconcile them into a supported opinion of value.

5

Receive Your Report

Your completed USPAP-compliant appraisal is delivered by secure download link, typically within three to five business days. Rush delivery is available for time-sensitive estate matters.
