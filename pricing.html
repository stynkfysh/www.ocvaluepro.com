What’s Included

A defensible opinion of fair market value as of the date of death, a description of the property and the analysis, the comparable sales relied upon, and a signed certification meeting USPAP requirements.

When the Fee May Be Higher

Complex, high-value, multi-unit, or unusually documented properties may be quoted above the starting rate. You always receive your exact fee in writing before any work begins.
